Millennial Falcon

Millennial Falcon is being created with the idea that living in our time has become too complicated and it needs to be simplified. MF's goal is to simplify Americans' understanding and interation with finance. From debt, income and lifetime planning to connecting with financial advisors, MF is here to bankrupt the fatcats by educating everyone for free!

austin.fwd.us

While studying at the Iron Yard I was lucky enough to attend my first FWD.us meetup. While at the tech industry shindig I was given the opportunity to build out the Austin chapter's site. Along with two other Iron Yarders (Gabe - Front End, Rob - Ruby on Rails) we were able to build the project for both FWD and the Iron Yard Hackathon we had over the weekend. In doing so, we learned a great amount about agile development as our production version (Iron Yard) had much different requirements than our distribution version (FWD). Overall, we had a great time building it out and loved the chance to work with other developers even if there were a few merge conflicts along the way.

  • Link: austin.fwd.us
  • Date: March 2015
