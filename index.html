About

Hughie is currently completing the Front End Engineering course at The Iron Yard in Austin, TX. After excelling as a financial and business analyst in the steel and wealth management industries for three years, he decided to further develop his interest in programming into a career and lifestyle focused on bettering the world through innovative and insightful software development.

Since embarking on his intensive education in front end engineering, Hugh has come to believe that the best development is done through concise planning and mindful execution. With his newly sharpened skills, Hughie is set to thrive in a career within the industry that has been the largest catalyst for socially impactful change in the 21st century, tech!

Upon graduation Hughie will be looking to utilize his programming abilities and experienced business acumen to bring creative and efficient programming to the role of front-end developer at a socially responsible tech company.
